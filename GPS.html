GPS

More Than Just A Movie . Learn More About GPS!

While the school students are going to the exhibition, the bus is stopped in the forest due to a breakdown. Some of the students there stray into the forest and are held by two smugglers .

It ends with the reasons why those smugglers hold the students and how they escape from the problem with the help of other students and what happened afterwards.

"From the very beginning we made it with the intention of making it a film that families and children would come to see in theaters and a film mixed with comedy that would create drug awareness among school students."

GPS Making Gallery

The shooting of the film took only one week. Because it was a one-day story like a survival thriller, it helped to complete the film very quickly.

Most of the actors are school kids, so we gave them acting training.

Most of the time had to be spent on their rehearsals. Apart from that, we enjoyed those shooting spot memories with the school kids.
