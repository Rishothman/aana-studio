OMEGA - The New Beginning

More Than Just A Movie . Learn More About OMEGA!

Omega travels through the Jayanthi & Siyamapriya characters.

Siyamapriya works in the media industry in Batticaloa. A marriage proposal comes to her from abroad after long time. But she refuses for one reason.

Jayanthi is suspicious of her husband's behavior. She tries many ways to solve this doubt. Her life takes a turn when she knows the dark secret from her husband.

It ends at a point with some branching stories as the reason why Siyamapriya refuses the marriage & Jayanthi's decision to turn her life around.

"It was written and created from the beginning with true stories and experiences with the aim of bringing the issues in society that are not noticed by the people."

OMEGA Making Gallery

The shooting of the film took place continuously for 3 months. Some actors and actresses from many parts of Sri Lanka performed.

Art work and pre-production work were already well planned, so the shoot was completed within the scheduled time frame without any difficulties.

Apart from that, we were able to keep the pressure and time management under control while shooting. More over Some scenes and dialogues were improvised during the shooting.
