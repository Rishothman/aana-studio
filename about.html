STUDIO

"Aana Studio Established in 2016, Aana Studio is a premier multimedia production house, crafting compelling short and feature films, documentaries, and evocative photography. With a robust portfolio boasting 18 short films and 2 feature films, we pride ourselves on merging audiovisual elements to tell captivating stories."

Learn More About STUDIO!

Our commitment extends beyond mere production; we collaborate with NGOs, delivering impactful documentaries that shed light on pressing societal issues. Our dedication to artistic growth resonates through specialized training, mentorship programs, and curated cultural events, fostering a thriving artistic community.

At the heart of Aana Studio lies a passion for artistic collaboration. We serve as a nexus for artists, transcending boundaries to cultivate an environment of innovation and experimentation. Through exhibitions, performances, and workshops, we champion both traditional and contemporary art forms, ensuring artists' voices resonate on a global stage.

Mission

Aana Studio offers specialized training for artists, fostering both traditional and contemporary art forms through mentorship, experimentation, and boundary-pushing workshops.

Vision

We foster artistic collaboration, uniting diverse artists to create a dynamic community. Through exhibitions and events, we amplify opportunities for artists to engage and showcase their work.
