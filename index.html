STUDIO

Aana Studio, founded in 2016, is an audiovisual production company specializing in multimedia content, including short films, feature films, documentaries, and photography. With a portfolio of 18 short films and 2 feature films, they've also partnered with local NGOs on seven documentary projects and produced music videos.

Aana Studio's mission is to create immersive and visually captivating content, offering a comprehensive range of production services. They provide training, mentorship, and foster artistic collaboration through exhibitions, performances, and cultural events, cultivating a vibrant artistic community.
